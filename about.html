ABOUT US

A family company since 1960

Montreal Tool & Equipment Rentals
Emco Machinery is a family run company, the Millers were known for their original trade on Fairmount named "Miller Paint". Eventually the business branched out into tool rentals and flooring supplies in their current landmark location at 6659 Park Avenue. Emco's knowledgeable employees have been servicing clientele for years, helping them learn how to use tools for the 1st time or repairing the commercial equipment for our local contractors.
Emco Machinery
A part of Montreal's history for over 50 years. Based in the "Mile-End" of Montreal, Emco Machinery has been accommodating the commercial and residential sectors of Montreal with tool rentals and flooring supplies since the 1960s.
Flooring Equipment Rental & Flooring Supplies
3 generations of Millers have been seen at the front desk operating Emco Machinery and guiding its growth. In 2011 Emco expanded its operations by acquiring more space in which to warehouse their extensive collection of tools and flooring supplies. Over the years Emco has become the "goto" guys for flooring equipment rental and flooring equipment supplies. From sanders to sandpaper, varnish and stains we have it all.
